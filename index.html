On this page

    Ismaili Studies Resources

    The purpose of this site is to collect and share resources for Ismaili Studies in one place for the benefit of the students and scholars of Ismaili history and societies. Digital and archival materials of relevance to Ismaili studies are vast, and thus not easy to navigate for novice and expert alike. It is hoped that this page will be useful in locating primary sources, manuscripts and documents, coins and objects, academic literature and new media, as well as digital tools of analysis. The reader we have in mind is anyone interested in the subject. Contributions are welcome.

    There are many other websites, online guides and other similar tools that help to navigate the broader fields which include Ismaili studies, such as Islamic Studies. Links to many of these will be given. Links to other major sources such Encylopaedia Islamica will also be given, but the goal is to bring together specialist resources that go under the radar of everyone else.
